  • Is Bangladesh the most beautiful country in the world?

    Bangladesh has flourished herself as a beautiful country around the world due to its unlimited natural beauty like the longest sea beach in Cox's bazaar, the largest mangrove forest & the royal Bengal tiger in Sundarbans. Also you can see the ancient archaeological site.

  • What is natural beauty of Bangladesh?

    Bangladesh has a land of enormous beauty, hundreds of serpentine rivers, crystal clear water lakes surrounded by ever green hills, luxuriant tropical rain forests, beautiful cascades of green tea gardens, world's largest mangrove forest preserved as World Heritage, home of the Royal Bengal Tiger.

  • What is the natural resources of Bangladesh?

    Although Bangladesh is a small country, it has a number of mineral resources such as natural gas, oil, coal, hard rock, limestone, white clay, glass sand and mineral sand. At present, natural gas is the only mineral commodity significantly contributing to the national economy.

  • What has made Bangladesh a land of heaven?

    Pristine sandy beaches, coconut palms, sunshine and tropical weather - Cox's Bazar in the south-eastern corner of Bangladesh has everything to make it an ideal holiday destination. ... This tropical paradise is key to the Bangladeshi government's new plans to put the country on the international tourist map.

  • What is famous food in Bangladesh?

    Rice, fish curry, and lentil is the most common traditional Bangladeshi food for general people. Bangladesh is also famous for its desserts. You will find dozens of sweets, rice cakes, rice puddings, and many other desserts in Bangladesh, mostly made from rice and cow milk. It is a paradise for food lovers.
